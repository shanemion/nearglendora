Mantra Coffee Co.

Though perhaps the most "indie" of the three here, Mantra delivers the coziest atmosphere. Offering a variety of seating, pastries, and teas and coffees, Mantra feels like home no matter the hour.

Classic Coffee

Located in the heart of Downtown Glendora, Classic is a, well, classic meeting spot for many. Whether it be for business, studying, or just a good time, the central location paired with both the indoor and outdoor seating are hard to beat.

Class One Donuts

A two minute drive away from Glendora High School, Class One Donuts is perfect for students looking for that early boost to their day. Whether it be with their strong, solid coffee or their cheap donut holes, Class One is an easy recommend for locals.
